Gemini governs communication, variety, duality, and mental agility. Her clients are drawn to her mind — her ability to articulate, explain, connect ideas, and adapt. They return because the conversation continues to evolve. Ideal client profile: curious, intellectually engaged, open to process.

Protective Factor

Saturn's presence in H7 (alongside Venus the 11th lord) is a long-term income protector. It means the income model, once established, becomes durable and resistant to market fluctuations. The key: build slowly, build relationships, and the structure becomes permanent.
